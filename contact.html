Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our catering services

What is the minimum guest count for catering?

Our minimum guest count is 25 people for full-service catering. For smaller gatherings, we offer drop-off catering options for groups as small as 10. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For corporate events and smaller gatherings, 4-6 weeks notice is ideal. However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests with 1-2 weeks notice depending on availability.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes! We offer complimentary tastings for events with 50+ guests. Tastings typically include 3-4 appetizers, 2-3 entrees, and 2 desserts from your selected package. Tastings must be scheduled in advance and are available Tuesday-Saturday.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We regularly accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, and other dietary needs. We can also prepare kosher and halal meals with advance notice. Just let us know your requirements when booking.

What's included in your catering packages?

Our packages include food preparation, delivery, setup, serving pieces, and basic cleanup. Premium and Luxury packages also include professional service staff, china/glassware, and linens. Additional services like bar service, rentals, and full event coordination are available as add-ons.

Do you provide bar service?

Yes! We offer full bar service including beer, wine, spirits, and signature cocktails. Our licensed bartenders can create custom drink menus for your event. We can provide the alcohol, or you can purchase your own and we'll provide the bartender and setup.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Northern New Jersey including Essex, Bergen, Morris, Passaic, Hudson, and Union counties. We can also travel to other areas of New Jersey and parts of New York for larger events. Travel fees may apply for events outside our primary service area.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require a 50% deposit to secure your date, which is non-refundable within 30 days of the event. Cancellations more than 30 days prior receive a 50% refund. Guest count adjustments can be made up to 7 days before the event. Full payment is due 7 days before the event date.
