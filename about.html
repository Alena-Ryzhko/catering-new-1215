Our Story

A journey of passion, dedication, and culinary excellence

From Humble Beginnings

Signature Events Catering was founded in 2010 by Chef Michael Romano and his wife, Elena, in a small commercial kitchen in Montclair, New Jersey. What started as a passion project quickly grew into one of the region's most sought-after catering companies.

Chef Michael, a graduate of the Culinary Institute of America, brought his fine dining expertise from renowned New York City restaurants to create innovative, memorable menus. Elena, with her background in event planning, ensured every detail was perfect.

Their combined vision was simple: exceptional food paired with extraordinary service. This philosophy continues to guide everything we do today.
